# Chapter 11.3: Humanoid Robotics Applications and Social Interaction

## 11.3.1 Introduction to Humanoid Applications

Humanoid robots represent a unique category of robotic systems designed to interact with humans and operate in human environments. Their applications span multiple domains, from research and development to practical service applications, with their human-like form factor enabling intuitive interaction and compatibility with human-designed spaces.

### 11.3.1.1 Application Categories
- **Service robotics**: Robots providing direct services to humans
- **Research platforms**: Experimental systems for scientific study
- **Educational tools**: Teaching and learning aids
- **Entertainment systems**: Interactive and engaging experiences

### 11.3.1.2 Market Segmentation
- **Commercial applications**: Business-focused robotic solutions
- **Healthcare applications**: Medical and therapeutic robots
- **Educational applications**: Academic and training robots
- **Research applications**: Scientific and experimental systems

## 11.3.14 Conclusion
